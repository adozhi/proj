Main directions of monetary policy

The main directions of the monetary policy of the Republic of Tajikistan are developed in accordance with Articles 4 and 14 of the Law of the Republic of Tajikistan "On the National Bank of Tajikistan" and define the main directions of the monetary and foreign exchange policy of the state, the effective use of monetary policy levers and the stable development of monetary indicators to achieve the main goal - maintaining a stable price level in the coming year.

The National Bank of Tajikistan annually submits the draft “Main Directions of the Monetary Policy of the Republic of Tajikistan” for consideration and approval to the Majlisi Namoyandagon of the Majlisi Oli of the Republic of Tajikistan no later than November 1.
