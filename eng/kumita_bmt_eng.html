About the Committee

The Monetary Policy Committee of the National Bank of Tajikistan was established as an independent, permanently operating advisory body in accordance with the Law of the Republic of Tajikistan "On the National Bank of Tajikistan", the Regulations of the Monetary Policy Committee and other regulatory legal acts of the National Bank of Tajikistan.

The activities of the Monetary Policy Committee (Committee) are aimed at making decisions related to the development and implementation of the country's monetary policy to maintain price stability in the long term, determining its parameters and priorities in regulating interest rates and maintaining equilibrium in the money market.

The composition of the Committee is approved by a decision of the Board of the National Bank of Tajikistan with an odd number of members.

The Chairman of the National Bank of Tajikistan serves as Chairman of the Committee.

The Committee submits quarterly reports on its activities to the Board of the National Bank of Tajikistan.

Main objectives of the Committee:

  • Adopting decisions on the main directions of monetary policy in order to develop and effectively implement monetary policy, and to achieve and maintain a stable level of domestic prices in the long term;
  • Reviewing analytical indicators of the macroeconomic, monetary and currency situation, and determining tasks and benchmark indicators for the implementation of monetary policy in the short and medium term;
  • Reviewing and adopting decisions on the implementation of one form or another of monetary policy (tight, moderate, stimulative) and its methods and instruments, depending on the current macroeconomic situation, the state of the external economy, inflationary factors and risks, as well as the forecast of key monetary and macroeconomic indicators;
  • In accordance with Article 15 of the Law of the Republic of Tajikistan "On the National Bank of Tajikistan", determining the priority, necessity and procedure for using monetary policy instruments in order to prevent and eliminate inflationary pressure, manage key monetary indicators, interest rates, and maintain equilibrium in the money and foreign exchange markets;
  • Coordinating the activities of structural divisions and observing rules in accordance with the established procedure in order to facilitate and ensure the transparency of the process of implementing monetary and foreign exchange policy, making decisions and achieving the main goal of the National Bank of Tajikistan.
