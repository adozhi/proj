Monetary Policy Committee of the National Bank of Tajikistan

The Monetary Policy Committee of the National Bank of Tajikistan was established as an independent, permanent advisory body in accordance with the Law of the Republic of Tajikistan “On the National Bank of Tajikistan”, the Regulation on the Monetary Policy Committee of the National Bank of Tajikistan and other regulatory and legal acts of the National Bank of Tajikistan.

The activities of the Monetary Policy Committee of the National Bank of Tajikistan (the Committee) are aimed at making decisions regarding the development and implementation of the country's monetary policy in order to maintain price stability in the long term, determine its parameters and priorities in regulating interest rates and maintaining equilibrium in the money market.

The composition of the Committee is approved by the decision of the Board of the National Bank of Tajikistan and provides for an odd number of members.

The Chairman of the National Bank of Tajikistan is the Chairman of the Committee.

The Committee submits a quarterly report on the results of its activities to the Council of the National Bank of Tajikistan.

The main tasks of the Committee:

  • Making decisions on the main directions of monetary policy with the aim of developing and effectively implementing monetary policy, achieving and maintaining a stable level of domestic prices in the long term;
  • Review of analytical indicators of the macroeconomic, monetary and foreign exchange situation, definition of objectives and benchmarks for the implementation of monetary policy in the short and medium term;
  • Consideration and decision-making on the implementation of one or another form of monetary policy (strict, moderate, stimulating), methods and levers (instruments) for its implementation depending on the current macroeconomic situation, the state of the external economy, inflationary factors and risks, as well as the forecast of key monetary and macroeconomic indicators;
  • In accordance with Article 15 of the Law of the Republic of Tajikistan “On the National Bank of Tajikistan”, which defines the priorities, necessity and procedure for using monetary policy instruments in order to prevent and eliminate inflationary foreign exchange pressure, manage key monetary indicators, interest rates, maintain equilibrium in the foreign exchange and currency markets, and also assigns tasks to other relevant structural divisions of the National Bank of Tajikistan for their implementation;
  • Coordination of the activities of structural divisions and compliance with the rules in accordance with the established procedure in order to facilitate and ensure transparency in the process of implementing monetary and foreign exchange policy, decision-making and achieving the main goal of the National Bank of Tajikistan.
